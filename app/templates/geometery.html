Angles

  • Angles are created with two lines
  • When to lines cross, they cross at an angle
  • Angles are measured in degrees
  • 360 degrees make a circle

Triangles

  • Triangles are shapes that have three sides
  • Because they have three sides, they have three angles
  • All three angles have to add up to 180 degrees

Graphing

  • A graph has two parts:
    • The X-Axis: numbers going from left to right
    • The Y-Axis: numbers going from left to right
  • An equation is given that you have to graph (Ex: y=2x+6)
  • The equation is always a relationship between an input, usually denoted using x, and the output, usually denoted as y
  • The most important thing to know is the slope of the function
    • The slope of the function determines how steep the line is
    • The slope is the coeffiecient of the x variable (Ex: Equation: y = 2x+6, Slope = 2)
