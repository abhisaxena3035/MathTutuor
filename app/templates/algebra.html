Variables

  • Variables are letters or symbols that hold a value
  • Yes, anything can be a variable
  • The main two variables used are x and y

Equations

  • Equations are a combination of:
    • Mathematical Operations and Symbols such as multiplication, addition, and equals sign
    • Variables that we need to solve for
    • Constants, or numbers, that are used to indicate or change value
  • Most of algebra is getting an equation and figuring out how to solve it
  • If you have to do something to one side of the equation, you have to do it to the other as well

Problem Solving Steps

  • Isolate the Variable
    • Add/Subtract constants on the side of the variable to get rid of it
  • Multiple/Divide to get rid of coefficients (the number in front of a variable. Ex: 2x/2)
  • Get the equation to look like the variable with no coefficeints to equal a single number
