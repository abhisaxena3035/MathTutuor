Derivatives

  • Derivatives is the graph of a function's slopes at tangent lines
  • Tangent lines touch the graph of the function at exactly one specific point
  • At each point on that function there is a tangent line, and the derivatives is the slope of the tangent line at that x value
  • You can take a derivative of every fucntion that doesn't have a discontinuity or sharp turn

Integrals

  • Integrals are a symbol to find the area under a curve
  • They can be definite or indefinite
    • Definite: They have bounds to take the area from where to where
    • Indefinite: You find the equation that finds the area of a function at different bounds
  • Integrals usually look like a large stretched out 'S' with a function after it
